Welcome to the Clew Bay Half-Marathon & 10k.

We are in our 7th year and and would love you to join us here in Louisburgh for a fun filled weekend!

Starting at 11am the Half-Marathon has prizes for 1st, 2nd, 3rd, male & female and prizes for over 40s male and over 40s female.

Starting at 11:30 am the 10k has prizes for 1st, 2nd, 3rd, male & female and prizes for over 40s male and over 40s females.

Delicious home made refreshments await you on completion of the race along with hot showers and changing room facilities
