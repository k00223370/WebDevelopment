2017 will be the 7th year in a row that we will be hosting the Clew Bay Half Marathon & 10K. Every year we have tried to improve the event by making it a bigger event and a more enjoyable experience for those taking part and those who volunteer.

Over the last few years we have improved by introducing the latest chip-timing technology, improving the quality of t-shirts and medals that all competitors receive, increasing and improving the marketing reach, improving prizes, improving the registration experience and overall trying to make the event day a great occasion for runners, volunteers and the community as a whole.
