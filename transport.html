Getting here

Flying

Flights from London Gatwick - Knock Airport daily.

For more information about transfers from Knock to Louisburgh Please Click Here

Dublin Airport serves as an excellent destination point for people travelling from anywhere in the world. Just half an hour from the airport, Iranrod Eireann will bring you by train to Westport where pre-booked taxi services will bring you on your final leg of the journey to Louisburgh

Train

Iranrod Eireann services most of Ireland and makes Louisburgh very accessible from Dublin with 5 train services daily to Westport Station. These trains depart Dublin at 7:55, 12:00, 14:00 and 18:05.

Bus

Bus Eireann offers three services from Westport to Louisburgh on a Saturday but does not run on a sunday
